About + Resume

I'm a senior at Penn State Behrend, majoring in Digital Media, Arts, and Technology with a minor in Game Development. I have a passion for digital creation, and I'm currently working as an Immersive Administrator at the Virtual and Augmented Reality (VAR) Lab at Penn State Behrend.

I'm always seeking ways to improve my skills, with the goal of working in an environment that values efficiency, creativity, and digital innovation. I'm eager to apply my knowledge to any job or business.

My work is driven by an interest in designing and developing practical, low-cost solutions to real problems. I focus on using accessible tools and thoughtful design to make technology more usable, sustainable, and impactful—especially for individuals, small organizations, and communities without access to large resources.

I grew up in Clarion County, Pennsylvania, but have strong hopes to explore, travel, and continue learning through new experiences.

If you'd like to know more about my work and experience, feel free to check out my resume below.
