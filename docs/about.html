About + Resume

I'm a Digital Media, Arts, and Technology graduate from Penn State Behrend with a minor in Game Development. My work focuses on immersive technology, 3D modeling, digital fabrication, interactive media, and practical design solutions.

During my time at Penn State Behrend, I worked as an Immersive Administrator at the Virtual and Augmented Reality (VAR) Lab, where I contributed to projects involving virtual reality, augmented reality, 3D scanning, 3D printing, interactive installations, research support, and digital preservation.

My work is driven by an interest in designing and developing practical, low-cost solutions to real problems. I focus on using accessible tools and thoughtful design to make technology more usable, sustainable, and impactful, especially for individuals, small organizations, and communities without access to large resources.

I enjoy projects that combine creativity with technical problem-solving, whether that means building interactive experiences, developing game systems, creating visual media, or using emerging technologies in new ways.

I grew up in Clarion County, Pennsylvania, and I hope to continue exploring, traveling, and learning through new experiences, both personally and professionally.

If you'd like to know more about my work and experience, feel free to email me at remingtonorange712@gmail.com or check out my resume below.
