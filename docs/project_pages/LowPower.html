Low Power – CGI Short Film

For my DIGIT 220N final, I created a short film about a robot in search of energy. This project is a CGI-driven short film that blends live-action footage I shot with digital assets I created, animated, and rendered in Blender.

The focus of the project was integrating CGI into real footage by matching lighting, scale, camera motion, and scene composition so the digital elements feel grounded within the environment.
