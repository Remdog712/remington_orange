Mint Condition Auto Detailing

Mint Condition Auto Detailing was a high school startup project developed in collaboration with the current owner, where I took on an informal cofounder role. I led the creation of the brand from the ground up, demonstrating initiative by establishing a complete visual identity and early marketing presence. This included designing the logo, defining the branding system, and shaping the overall aesthetic to reflect a clean, high-end automotive finish. I also created promotional graphics and social media content to ensure a consistent, professional look that could scale as the business grew. Since its launch, Mint Condition has serviced over 1,000 vehicles and expanded its offerings beyond basic detailing into a broader range of automotive services, reflecting the strength and longevity of the brand foundation.
