PandoAmerica

This video was created for a Game 180 class which focuses on 3D World building, geography, bodies of water, and fauna. My inspiration for this came from an North American adapation of James Cameron's Avatar's Pandora. My name isn't clever, but once the link between Avatar and the project are established, it's easier to understand the color and lighting choices. Everything was created in Blender (some assets obtained through Blenderkit) and edited together in Adobe After Effects
