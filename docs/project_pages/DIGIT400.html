Blender + Substance Painter (Three.js)

This project showcases 3D models that I created and animated in Blender, with all texturing completed using Adobe Substance Painter. The finished assets were then deployed to the web using Three.js for real-time interaction.

Each model was inspired by historical illustrations from artist Filippo Morghen, sourced via Artvee.com. Morghen was an Italian printmaker and engraver, born in Florence and working in Naples from circa 1760. Much of his work depicted the antiquities of Italy, including architecture, frescoes, statues, and ruins.

The original illustrations come from the book Suite of the most notable things seen by John Wilkins erudite English Bishop during his famous voyage from the Earth to the Moon, which served as the visual foundation for translating 2D engravings into fully realized 3D environments.
