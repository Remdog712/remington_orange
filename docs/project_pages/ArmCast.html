3D Printed Arm Cast Concept

This project explores a low-cost, 3D-printed arm cast concept designed to help children or adults temporarily limit use of their stronger arm, encouraging targeted practice and strength-building with a weaker arm. The prints and design iterations are being developed with the intent of supporting a future case study on affordable, accessible rehabilitation-adjacent solutions—focused on practicality, comfort, and repeatable fabrication.
